Cloud & Application Services

Leveraging the cloud today is no longer simply rehosting applications, but instead intelligent and thoughtful steps towards engineering cloud-native systems

Modern cloud platforms are complex.

As an Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Platform partner, we have significant experience in migrating applications, data and infrastructure to the cloud; delivering potentially significant total cost of ownership, resiliency, scalability, and security benefits.

This is a natural extension of our disaggregation, service design, and service management capabilities for a full end-to-end service, designed to expand your operating model to place Cloud-Native engineering at the heart of capability and service development.

We place emphasis… 

…on cloud interoperability and integration ensuring you get cloud transformation right the first time. Our technical architects and engineers help build and connect sustainable cloud-native systems to thrive in the cloud ecosystem. Services include cloud-native software development, container deployment, serverless platforms, landing zones, automation orchestration and configuration management, continuous integration/deployments, custom microservices, and API-based solutions.

We’ll help you…

…enable DevOps and DevSecOps-aligned software and platform engineering capabilities based on continuous integration/delivery principles. We’ll also work with you to coach and transfer knowledge to your in-house teams, so they can take ownership of operating and improving your services.

Our work…

…is based on modern, well-architected framework and ‘Secure Software Development Life Cycle’ principles, delivering secure, high-performing, resilient, well-monitored, and cost-effective platforms for your applications and data.

Our expertise within cloud engineering

  • Cloud automation and DevSecOps
  • Cloud landing zones and virtual data centres
  • Cloud governance and reporting
  • Container orchestration and management
  • Cloud integration platforms
  • Hybrid and multi-cloud strategy
  • Continuous integration / delivery principles

Taking an organisation on the path to transformation, with one or more cloud service providers, starts with laying the right foundation in terms of the cloud platform. The cloud platform streamlines the process of adopting cloud services through a well-defined catalogue that meets organisational process, compliance, and operational needs.
